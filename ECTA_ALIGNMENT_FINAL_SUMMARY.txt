SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================

5-STAGE REGISTRATION PROCESS:
Stage 1 → Stage 2 → Stage 3 → Stage 4 → Stage 5 → READY FOR EXPORT
(5-7d)   (1-2w)   (2-3w)   (1-2w)   (1w)    (4-6 months total)

8-PHASE EXPORT WORKFLOW:
Phase 1: ECX Verification (2-3 days)
Phase 2: ECTA Regulatory Approval (1-2 weeks)
Phase 3: Commercial Bank Verification (2-3 days)
Phase 4: National Bank FX Approval (2-3 days)
Phase 5: Export Customs Clearance (1-2 days)
Phase 6: Shipment & Transport (2-4 weeks)
Phase 7: Import Customs & Delivery (2-3 days)
Phase 8: Payment & Completion (1-2 weeks)
Total: 4-8 weeks

ONGOING COMPLIANCE:
- Quarterly Reporting (ECTA-REP-001)
- Annual Audits (ECTA-AUD-001)
- Facility Inspections (ECTA-INS-001)
- Violation Tracking (ECTA-VIO-001)
